*BETA* ultraPacer Embeddable Table Demo

Just insert the following into your document head:

<script src="https://ultrapacer.com/public/components/js/up-table.min.js"></script>

And then put this on the page where you want it:

<up-table
 course-id="5dc742995f5b9b0007339dfd"
 columns="location,name,elevation,distance,gain"
 style="max-width:400px"
/>
